The bull made legitimate points that deserve weight: $39.7M in fresh contracts across three distinct growth vectors in four weeks is a genuine data shift; the $21.5M defense follow-on is real production revenue, not speculation; the Fabric.AI development contract — whatever the counterparty risk — is a paying customer validating the MicroLED optical interconnect architecture in an unavoidable AI infrastructure end-market; and two Wall Street firms have reinstated coverage with Buy ratings. Netting it out: the transformation thesis is live but far from proven, and the stock has already priced in a substantial chunk of the best-case outcome. The asymmetry that existed at $1.81-$3.00 no longer exists at $5.41. New money should not chase here; existing exposure should be reduced to reflect the deteriorated risk/reward. This is a classic Underweight — lean bearish on price, stay open to re-engaging on a meaningful, fundamentally-confirmed pullback.
