Money is moving into risk assets, and AAPL benefits as a primary beneficiary. That distinction matters for sizing.

**The middle ground:** Use institutional flows as a *context* signal, not a conviction signal. The macro backdrop supports risk-on, but your AAPL entry needs its own fundamental justification.
