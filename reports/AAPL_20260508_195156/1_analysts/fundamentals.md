## 8. Risk Factors & Concerns

1. **Valuation Premium**: PEG of 2.52 is elevated. Any earnings miss could trigger significant downside.
2. **China Exposure**: Regulatory risks and geopolitical tensions remain a concern for hardware sales.
3. **Antitrust Scrutiny**: Ongoing global regulatory pressure on App Store practices and market dominance.
4. **Consumer Electronics Cyclicality**: iPhone upgrade cycles are lengthening; market saturation in developed economies.
5. **Revenue Concentration**: Heavy reliance on iPhone (~50%+ of revenue historically).
6. **Debt Load**: $84.7B total debt, though manageable given cash flows.

## 9. Competitive Advantages (Moat Assessment)

- ✅ **Supply chain mastery**: Vertical integration (M-series/M-series chips, proprietary silicon)

- ✅ **Shareholder returns**: Consistent buybacks + dividend create shareholder loyalty
