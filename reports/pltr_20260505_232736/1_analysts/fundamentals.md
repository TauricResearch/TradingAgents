## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Palantir Technologies Inc. (PLTR)** is a leading U.S.-based data analytics and artificial intelligence software company headquartered in Denver, Colorado. The company develops platforms for big data analytics and AI-powered decision-making, primarily serving government agencies and large commercial enterprises. It operates in two primary segments: **Government** and **Commercial**.

- **Sector:** Technology
- **Industry:** Software – Infrastructure
- **Market Capitalization:** ~$325.8 billion

## 9. 🔎 Key Risks & Considerations

1. **Valuation Risk:** P/E of 154x (TTM) and Price/Book of 44x leave little room for error. Any slowdown in growth could trigger a sharp devaluation.
2. **Stock-Based Compensation (SBC):** While declining as a % of revenue, SBC (~$196M/quarter) remains a substantial real economic cost.
3. **Concentration Risk:** PLTR still has significant reliance on large government contracts; geopolitical or budget changes could impact revenue.
4. **Competition:** Intensifying AI competition from Microsoft, Google, Amazon, and pure-play AI firms.
5. **Current Price vs. Moving Averages:** Trading below 200-day MA (~$164.26) but above the 52-week low. Technically the stock may be in a consolidation phase.
6. **Dilution:** Share count has grown from 2.27B (Q4 2024) to 2.39B (Q4 2025), a meaningful ~5.3% dilution.
