## 1. 🏢 Company-Specific News: Palantir (PLTR)

### Q1 2026 Earnings: Beat Across the Board, But Stock Sold Off
Palantir delivered a **blowout Q1 2026 earnings report**, exceeding Wall Street estimates on every major metric:

- **Adjusted EPS:** $0.33 (vs. $0.28 expected) — a **~18% beat**
- **Revenue:** $1.63 billion (vs. $1.54 billion expected) — a **~5.8% beat**
- **Adjusted operating margin** and **adjusted free cash flow margin** also beat expectations
- **U.S. government revenue** was a key strength driver

Despite these impressive results, **PLTR stock fell approximately 6.9% on Tuesday, May 5**, as Wall Street absorbed the results. This "sell the news" reaction is a significant and telling signal.

### Why Did the Stock Fall Despite the Beat?
Multiple analyst sources cite **two key concerns**:

1. **Commercial segment miss:** While government sales were strong, Palantir's **commercial business disappointed** relative to elevated expectations. Analysts had priced in broader commercial AI platform traction, which appears to have fallen short.
2. **Extreme valuation:** At a current price of approximately **$146.03**, PLTR trades at a significant premium. The stock is **down ~13% year-to-date** and has already seen a **34.1% return over the past year** and a **6x return over 5 years**. The market appears to be questioning whether growth justifies the multiple — especially with **rising competition** in the AI/data analytics space.
3. **Capacity constraints:** Zacks flagged that capacity limitations and heightened competition create execution risk going forward even as AI demand surges.

### Price Action Context

- **-1.6% over 30 days**
- **-13.0% year-to-date**
- The stock is trading well off its 52-week highs
