## 1. 🔍 Instrument Overview: What is `gdxu`?

`gdxu` is the **MicroSectors Gold Miners Index 3X Leveraged ETN**, issued by Bank of Montreal (BMO). It aims to deliver **3x the daily performance** of the NYSE Arca Gold Miners Index (GDM), which tracks major gold and silver mining companies such as Newmont, Barrick Gold, and Agnico Eagle. As a **3x leveraged product**, `gdxu` is highly sensitive to:

- **Spot gold prices** (the primary revenue driver of mining companies)
- **Real interest rates and USD strength**
- **Macroeconomic uncertainty / risk-off sentiment**

## 4. ⚡ Leveraged ETN-Specific Risk Factors

`gdxu`'s **3x leverage** introduces critical structural considerations for any trading decision:

1. **Volatility Decay (Beta Slippage):** In choppy or sideways markets, the daily rebalancing of a 3x product erodes NAV over time, even if the underlying index is flat.
2. **High Beta to Gold Spot:** A 1% move in gold spot can translate to a **6–9% swing in `gdxu`** on an amplified basis.
3. **Short-Term Trading Only:** These instruments are explicitly designed for **intraday or very short-term trading**, not long-term holding.
4. **Counterparty Risk:** As an ETN (not ETF), `gdxu` carries BMO credit risk.
