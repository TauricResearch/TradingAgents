- **RSI: 72.21**

RSI above 70 typically signals overbought conditions. In a strong trend, RSI can stay elevated for extended periods, so this should not be read as an automatic sell. Instead, it means:

- and the probability of a short-term pause or pullback is elevated.

The key nuance here is that RSI is overbought **while trend and MACD are still bullish**. That is usually more consistent with an extended uptrend than an imminent top. Still, it does mean chasing here is less attractive than buying a pullback.
