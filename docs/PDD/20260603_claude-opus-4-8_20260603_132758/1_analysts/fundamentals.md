## 1. COMPANY PROFILE

**PDD Holdings Inc.** is one of the world's largest e-commerce conglomerates, operating through its flagship platforms **Pinduoduo** (China domestic) and **Temu** (international markets). Pinduoduo pioneered the "team purchase" model in China, enabling consumers to collaborate for lower prices, while Temu has rapidly become one of the most downloaded shopping apps globally, targeting value-conscious consumers in the United States, Europe, and emerging markets.

PDD is headquartered in Dublin, Ireland (holding company), with principal operations in China. Shares trade as **American Depositary Shares (ADS)** on the Nasdaq under the ticker **PDD**. The company derives revenue primarily from **online marketing services** (merchants advertising on its platforms) and **transaction services** (commissions and logistics fees).
