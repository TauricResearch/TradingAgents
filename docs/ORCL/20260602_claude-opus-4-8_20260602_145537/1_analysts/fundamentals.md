## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Overview

**Oracle Corporation (ORCL)** is the world's second-largest software company by revenue and a dominant player in enterprise technology. Founded in 1977 and headquartered in Austin, Texas, Oracle provides database software, cloud infrastructure (Oracle Cloud Infrastructure — OCI), enterprise applications (ERP, HCM, SCM via Oracle Fusion), and managed cloud services. The company serves virtually every major industry vertical globally and competes directly with Microsoft Azure, Amazon Web Services, Google Cloud, SAP, and Salesforce.

Oracle's transformation from an on-premises database vendor to a full-stack cloud hyperscaler is in **full acceleration** — OCI is growing faster than any of the Big Three cloud providers (AWS, Azure, GCP) on a percentage basis, and the company has become a key AI infrastructure beneficiary via partnerships with NVIDIA and Microsoft.
