## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Overview

Oracle Corporation is one of the world's largest enterprise software and cloud computing companies. Founded in 1977 and headquartered in Austin, Texas, Oracle offers a broad suite of products including cloud infrastructure (OCI), cloud applications (ERP, HCM, SCM, CX), databases, and legacy on-premise solutions. The company competes directly with Microsoft Azure, Amazon AWS, Salesforce, SAP, and Workday across its various product verticals.

Oracle's strategic pivot to cloud — especially **Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI)** — is the defining theme of its recent financial trajectory. This transformation has required enormous capital investment but is beginning to yield substantial revenue acceleration.
