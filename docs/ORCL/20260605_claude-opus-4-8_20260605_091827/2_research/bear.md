**Goodwill impairment testing requires that the carrying value be recoverable from future cash flows.** If Oracle's healthcare division — facing documented implementation delays, hospital dissatisfaction, and integration complexity — cannot demonstrate those cash flows in the next one to two annual impairment cycles, Oracle faces a goodwill write-down. The bull calls this an "option." In options terminology, an option has limited downside (the premium paid). Goodwill impairment risk is not limited downside. It's a balance sheet bomb sitting on top of an already-extreme leverage profile.
