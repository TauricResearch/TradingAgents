5. **FCF Compression:** FCF fell to $10.1B in Q1 2026 from $19B in Q1 2025 — if CapEx remains elevated without a corresponding ramp in cloud/AI revenue, this could pressure the stock.
6. **M&A Integration Risk:** A $33.6B acquisition introduces integration complexity and goodwill impairment risk.
7. **Normalized Earnings vs. Reported:** Q1 2026 reported EPS of $5.11 was massively boosted by one-time security gains. Normalized EPS was ~$2.67, which is slightly below Q1 2025's $2.81 normalized. Investors need to distinguish between reported and core earnings.
