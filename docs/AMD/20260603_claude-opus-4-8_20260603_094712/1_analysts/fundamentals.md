## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (AMD)** is a global semiconductor company headquartered in Santa Clara, California. AMD designs and manufactures high-performance computing, graphics, and visualization technologies for data center, PC, gaming, and embedded markets. The company competes directly with Intel (CPUs), NVIDIA (GPUs/AI accelerators), and various embedded/FPGA peers following its 2022 acquisition of Xilinx.

AMD's product portfolio spans:
- **Data Center:** EPYC server CPUs, Instinct AI GPUs/accelerators, Pensando DPUs
- **Client:** Ryzen desktop and mobile processors
- **Gaming:** Radeon GPUs, semi-custom SoCs for Sony PlayStation and Microsoft Xbox
- **Embedded:** FPGAs, SoCs, adaptive computing (Xilinx legacy)
