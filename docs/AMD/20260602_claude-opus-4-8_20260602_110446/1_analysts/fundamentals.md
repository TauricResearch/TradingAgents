## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (AMD)** is a global semiconductor company headquartered in Santa Clara, California. AMD designs high-performance computing and visualization products, including CPUs (Central Processing Units), GPUs (Graphics Processing Units), FPGAs, and adaptive computing solutions. Its key product families include EPYC server processors, Ryzen CPUs, Radeon GPUs, and Instinct AI accelerators. AMD competes directly with Intel (CPUs), NVIDIA (GPUs/AI accelerators), and others across data center, consumer, gaming, and embedded markets.
