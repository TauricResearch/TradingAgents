## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (AMD) is a global semiconductor company headquartered in Santa Clara, California. AMD designs high-performance computing and visualization products, including CPUs (Ryzen, EPYC), GPUs (Radeon, Instinct), FPGAs, and adaptive SoCs. The company competes primarily with Intel (CPUs), NVIDIA (GPUs/AI accelerators), and Xilinx (now part of AMD). AMD has strategically positioned itself at the center of the AI, data center, gaming, and edge computing megatrends.
