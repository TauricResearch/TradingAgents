## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Business Overview

Meta Platforms, Inc. (NASDAQ: META) is the world's largest social media and digital advertising conglomerate. The company operates the globe's most-used family of applications — **Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Messenger** — serving billions of users daily. Meta's revenue is almost entirely derived from digital advertising sold across these platforms.

Meta is also aggressively investing in its **Reality Labs** division, encompassing augmented reality (AR), virtual reality (VR), and the metaverse. The company's dual-pronged strategy of monetizing its advertising behemoth while constructing next-generation computing platforms makes it a unique long-term technology bet. Meta has also made significant investments in AI infrastructure, including proprietary large language models (LLaMA series) and AI-powered content and ad targeting.
