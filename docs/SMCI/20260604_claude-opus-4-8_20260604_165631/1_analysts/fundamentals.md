## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Business Overview

Super Micro Computer, Inc. (SMCI) is a global leader in high-performance, high-efficiency server and storage technology solutions. The company designs, develops, manufactures, and sells server hardware, including complete servers, storage systems, modular blade servers, networking devices, server management software, and server subsystems. SMCI has become a central beneficiary of the AI infrastructure buildout, supplying GPU-dense server platforms — including NVIDIA-based AI clusters and liquid-cooled AI rack solutions — to hyperscalers, cloud providers, and enterprise customers worldwide.

**Key business drivers:**
- Strong positioning in the AI server market (NVIDIA GPU servers)
- Direct liquid cooling (DLC) technology for high-density AI workloads
- Broad product customization capability vs. traditional OEM competitors
- Expanding total addressable market driven by generative AI infrastructure spending
