## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

Hewlett Packard Enterprise Company (HPE) is a global enterprise technology company headquartered in Spring, Texas. Spun off from the original Hewlett-Packard Company in November 2015, HPE focuses on providing servers, storage, networking, edge computing, and as-a-service solutions to businesses worldwide. Its primary business divisions include:
- **Hybrid IT** (servers, storage, networking infrastructure)
- **Intelligent Edge** (Aruba networking)
- **Financial Services** (technology financing)
- **HPE GreenLake** (cloud-as-a-service hybrid cloud platform)

A landmark development in HPE's recent history is the **acquisition of Juniper Networks**, which closed in FY2026 (evidenced by a $12.278B acquisition cash outflow in Q3 FY2025 and a significant jump in goodwill from ~$18B to ~$23.8B). This transformational deal fundamentally reshapes HPE's networking and AI-driven infrastructure capabilities, but also dramatically altered its balance sheet.
