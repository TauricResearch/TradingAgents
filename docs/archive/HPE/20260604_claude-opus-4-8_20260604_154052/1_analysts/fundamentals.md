## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) is a global technology company that was spun off from Hewlett-Packard in November 2015. HPE provides IT infrastructure products and services including servers, storage, networking equipment, and hybrid cloud solutions. Key business segments include:

- **Compute (Server/HPC/AI):** Industry-standard servers and high-performance computing platforms
- **Storage:** Enterprise storage solutions and data management
- **Intelligent Edge (Networking/Aruba):** Network switching, wireless, and security solutions
- **Hybrid Cloud & Financial Services:** Cloud computing, software-defined infrastructure, and IT financing

A landmark event for HPE was the **completed acquisition of Juniper Networks** (closed in Q3 FY2025, reflected in Q3 2025 10-Q), substantially expanding its networking portfolio and significantly increasing revenue, total assets, goodwill, and debt. This acquisition fundamentally reshapes HPE's competitive positioning in the networking market.
