## 1. Company Profile & Overview

**Vistra Corp. (VST)** is one of the largest integrated retail electricity and power generation companies in the United States. Headquartered in Irving, Texas, Vistra operates across competitive and regulated electricity markets, with a diversified fleet of power generation assets including **natural gas, nuclear, coal, solar, and battery storage**. The company serves millions of retail electricity customers primarily through its TXU Energy and Dynegy brands.

Vistra plays a pivotal role in the energy transition narrative — its nuclear fleet and increasing investment in storage and renewables position it as a beneficiary of surging U.S. power demand driven by data centers, AI infrastructure buildout, and electrification trends.

- **Market Capitalization:** ~$51.86 billion
