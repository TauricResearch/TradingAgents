## 1. 🏭 Company Overview
**Lam Research Corporation (NASDAQ: LRCX)** is a leading provider of wafer fabrication equipment (WFE) and services to the global semiconductor industry. The company is a critical enabler of chip manufacturing across NAND flash, DRAM, high bandwidth memory (HBM), logic, and advanced packaging — all segments experiencing strong secular tailwinds from AI infrastructure buildout.

Archer framed AI spending as creating a sustainable multi-year equipment supercycle — particularly strong for Lam given its dominant positions in etch and deposition tools used in HBM and NAND.
