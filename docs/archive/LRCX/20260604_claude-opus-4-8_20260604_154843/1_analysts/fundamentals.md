2. **Semiconductor Cyclicality:** The industry is inherently cyclical (FY2024 revenue was ~15% below FY2023 peak). Another downcycle could rapidly compress revenues and margins.
3. **Elevated Valuation:** A TTM P/E of 63.6x leaves limited room for disappointment. Any guidance cuts or macro deterioration could trigger significant multiple compression.
4. **Concentration Risk:** High dependence on a handful of large customers (Samsung, SK Hynix, Micron, TSMC, Intel) for a majority of revenue.
5. **High Beta (1.82):** LRCX amplifies broader market moves, creating outsized drawdown risk in risk-off environments.
6. **Working Capital Volatility:** Large swings in receivables and deferred revenue can distort quarterly FCF readings.
7. **CapEx Acceleration:** Rising capital expenditure ($332M in latest quarter vs. ~$172–185M prior) may compress near-term FCF if revenue growth slows.
