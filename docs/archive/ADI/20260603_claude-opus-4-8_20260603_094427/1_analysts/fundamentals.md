## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Overview

**Analog Devices, Inc. (ADI)** is one of the world's leading high-performance semiconductor companies, specializing in data conversion, signal processing, and power management solutions. Founded in 1965 and headquartered in Wilmington, Massachusetts, ADI designs and manufactures integrated circuits (ICs) used in industrial automation, communications infrastructure, automotive systems, healthcare, and consumer electronics. The company's 2021 acquisition of Maxim Integrated significantly expanded its product portfolio and scale. ADI serves a highly diversified, blue-chip customer base across 125+ countries, underpinning remarkable revenue stability through economic cycles.
