## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Vishay Intertechnology, Inc.** is a global manufacturer and supplier of discrete semiconductors (diodes, MOSFETs, optoelectronics) and passive electronic components (resistors, capacitors, inductors). Founded in 1962 and headquartered in Malvern, Pennsylvania, Vishay serves customers across automotive, industrial, computing, telecommunications, medical, and military/aerospace end markets. The company operates manufacturing facilities worldwide, with significant operations in Asia, Europe, and the Americas.

Vishay is one of the largest manufacturers of passive electronic components globally, operating in a capital-intensive, cyclical industry. Its broad product portfolio and diversified global customer base differentiate it from pure-play semiconductor peers.
