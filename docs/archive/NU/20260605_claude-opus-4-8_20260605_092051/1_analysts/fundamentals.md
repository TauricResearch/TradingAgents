## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Overview

**Nu Holdings Ltd.** is a leading Latin American digital financial services platform, headquartered in the Cayman Islands with primary operations in **Brazil, Mexico, and Colombia**. Founded in 2013, Nu (operating under the Nubank brand) has grown into one of the largest digital banks in the world by customer count. The company operates a fully digital model with no physical branches, offering credit cards, personal loans, savings accounts, insurance, and investment products to consumers and small businesses.

Nu went public on the **NYSE (ticker: NU)** in December 2021 and has since demonstrated extraordinary revenue growth, transitioning from losses to sustained profitability. Its platform-native, low-cost operating model enables a structurally superior cost base compared to traditional Latin American incumbents.
