## 1. Company Profile & Overview

Nu Holdings Ltd. is a leading Latin American digital financial services platform headquartered in the Cayman Islands, with primary operations in Brazil, Mexico, and Colombia. Founded in 2013 by David Vélez, Cristina Junqueira, and Edward Wible, Nubank is among the world's largest digital banking platforms by customer count. The company went public on the NYSE in December 2021 (ticker: `NU`).

Nu operates a mobile-first, asset-light model, offering credit cards, personal loans, savings accounts, insurance, investment products, and more—without traditional brick-and-mortar infrastructure. Its competitive advantage rests on extremely low customer acquisition costs, a superior digital user experience, and a platform approach that allows rapid cross-selling across a massive and growing user base across Latin America.
