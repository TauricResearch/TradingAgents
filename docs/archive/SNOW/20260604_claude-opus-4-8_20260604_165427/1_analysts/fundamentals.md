## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Overview

**Snowflake Inc.** is a cloud-based data warehousing, data sharing, and analytics platform company. It operates a consumption-based business model, meaning customers are charged based on actual compute and storage usage rather than fixed subscription fees. This distinguishes Snowflake from traditional SaaS vendors and creates both high scalability and inherent revenue variability.

Snowflake's platform enables organizations to consolidate data, run analytics, share data across clouds, and increasingly leverage AI/ML workloads. The company competes with hyperscalers (AWS Redshift, Google BigQuery, Azure Synapse) and specialized data platforms (Databricks, Teradata, etc.).

- **CEO:** Sridhar Ramaswamy (appointed February 2024)
- **Fiscal Year:** Ends January 31
- **Market Cap (as of analysis date):** ~$84.6 billion
