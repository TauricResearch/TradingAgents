## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Overview

**Snowflake Inc.** is a cloud-native data platform company that provides a fully managed, multi-cloud data warehousing, data lake, and analytics solution. The platform enables organizations to store, process, and analyze data across Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform (GCP) — simultaneously on a single platform. Snowflake's business model is consumption-based, meaning customers pay for the compute and storage resources they actually use. The company has expanded its platform to include data sharing, the Snowflake Marketplace, and an increasingly important AI/ML and application development layer ("Snowpark," "Cortex AI").
