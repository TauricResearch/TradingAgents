## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Overview

**Marvell Technology, Inc. (MRVL)** is a leading fabless semiconductor company headquartered in Santa Clara, California. The company designs and develops a broad portfolio of mixed-signal ICs (integrated circuits), including data storage controllers, networking chips, custom silicon (ASICs), and high-speed interconnects. MRVL has strategically repositioned itself as a premier AI infrastructure semiconductor provider, with strong exposure to custom AI accelerators (XPUs), cloud data center networking (PCIe switches, coherent DSPs), and electro-optics.

**Key Strategic Pillars:**
- **Custom Silicon (AI XPUs):** Deepening partnerships with major hyperscalers (Amazon AWS, Google, Microsoft) for bespoke AI chip designs
- **Data Center Networking:** Teralynx Ethernet switching, PAM4 DSPs, and coherent optics
- **Electro-Optics / EO:** Enabling ultra-high bandwidth optical interconnects
- **Carrier Infrastructure:** 5G wireless processors and network access chips
