## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Marvell Technology, Inc. (MRVL)** is a leading semiconductor company headquartered in Santa Clara, California, specializing in data infrastructure semiconductor solutions. The company designs, develops, and sells integrated circuits and related software, serving data center, carrier infrastructure, enterprise networking, and automotive/industrial end markets. Marvell has established itself as a critical supplier to hyperscale cloud providers and AI infrastructure builders, with a strong and growing custom ASIC (Application-Specific Integrated Circuit) program for AI accelerators.

**Key Business Segments:**
- **Data Center:** Custom AI ASICs, PCIe retimers, storage controllers, Ethernet switches, and SerDes IP
- **Carrier Infrastructure:** Networking processors and optical DSPs for 5G buildouts
- **Enterprise Networking:** Ethernet controllers and switches for enterprise environments
- **Automotive/Industrial:** Embedded processors and networking solutions
