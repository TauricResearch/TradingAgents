## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

The surge in marketing/SG&A spend without commensurate revenue acceleration raises questions about capital allocation efficiency. Regulatory and geopolitical overhang remains persistently unresolved.

- ⚖️ **Balanced View:** BABA is in a **high-investment transition phase** — sacrificing near-term cash flow for long-term positioning in AI, cloud, and international commerce. The thesis hinges on whether the capex cycle generates returns. Traders with a **medium-to-long term horizon (12-24 months)** may find valuation metrics compelling. Short-term traders should be cautious given technical weakness (stock below 200DMA) and negative FCF trends.
