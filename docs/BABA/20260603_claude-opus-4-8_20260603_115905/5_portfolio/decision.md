Ambiguous prints (e.g., 2-point cloud acceleration, favorably-timed comparisons) do not clear the threshold. Size in the middle third of benchmark so a drawdown to the ~$75 bear scenario is portfolio-survivable while the mechanical stop — not size reduction — remains the primary behavioral protection.
