## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Overview

**Broadcom Inc. (AVGO)** is a global technology leader in semiconductors and infrastructure software. The company designs, develops, and supplies a broad range of semiconductor, enterprise software, and security solutions. Its product portfolio spans data center networking, broadband, wireless communications, storage adapters, and custom AI accelerators (XPUs). Broadcom dramatically expanded its software presence via the landmark **$69 billion acquisition of VMware** (closed November 2023), making it one of the largest enterprise software vendors alongside its chip business.
