## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Broadcom Inc. (AVGO)** is a global technology leader in semiconductor and infrastructure software solutions. Headquartered in Palo Alto, California, Broadcom designs, develops, and supplies a broad range of semiconductor and software products across data center networking, broadband, wireless communications, storage adapters, industrial, enterprise, and cloud infrastructure. The company underwent a transformational acquisition of **VMware** (closed November 2023) for approximately $69 billion, cementing its position as a major enterprise software powerhouse alongside its core semiconductor operations.

**Business Segments:**
- **Semiconductor Solutions:** Networking ASICs, custom AI accelerators (XPUs), broadband chips, Wi-Fi/Bluetooth, storage controllers, industrial semiconductors
- **Infrastructure Software:** VMware Cloud Foundation (VCF), mainframe and security software; post-VMware acquisition, now the dominant revenue contributor
