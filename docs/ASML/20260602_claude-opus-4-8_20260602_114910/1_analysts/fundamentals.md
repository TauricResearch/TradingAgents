## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Business Overview

**ASML Holding N.V.** is a Dutch multinational corporation headquartered in Veldhoven, Netherlands, and a global monopoly supplier of **photolithography systems** used in semiconductor manufacturing. ASML is the world's sole supplier of **Extreme Ultraviolet (EUV) lithography machines**, which are indispensable for producing chips at 7nm and below process nodes. The company also sells Deep Ultraviolet (DUV) systems and offers metrology/inspection tools through its Holistic Lithography platform.

Key competitive moats:
- **EUV monopoly** — no other company manufactures EUV scanners
- Massive R&D investment (≈14–15% of revenue annually)
- Deep integration into customers' production processes (TSMC, Samsung, Intel)
- Huge backlog driven by global chip capacity expansion
- Installed base services (recurring revenue stream)
