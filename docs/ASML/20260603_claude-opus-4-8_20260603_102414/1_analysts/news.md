## 1. 🏢 Company Overview

**ASML Holding N.V.** (ticker: `ASML`) is the world's sole manufacturer of extreme ultraviolet (EUV) lithography machines — the critical equipment used to manufacture the most advanced semiconductors. This monopoly position makes ASML an indispensable node in the global semiconductor supply chain, with no viable competitor in the EUV space. The company is headquartered in Veldhoven, Netherlands and trades on both NASDAQ (ASML) and Euronext Amsterdam (ASME).
