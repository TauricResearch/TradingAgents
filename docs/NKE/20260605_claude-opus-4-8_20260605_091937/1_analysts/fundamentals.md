## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Overview

**NIKE, Inc.** is the world's largest designer, developer, marketer, and seller of athletic footwear, apparel, equipment, and accessories. Operating globally across North America, Europe, Middle East & Africa (EMEA), Greater China, and Asia Pacific & Latin America (APLA), Nike commands an unmatched portfolio of brands including Nike, Jordan Brand, and Converse. Nike's business model is heavily weighted toward direct-to-consumer (DTC) channels and a growing e-commerce platform. Its fiscal year ends on **May 31**.
