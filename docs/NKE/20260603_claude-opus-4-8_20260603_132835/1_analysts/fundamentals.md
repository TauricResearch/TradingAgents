## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**NIKE, Inc.** is the world's largest athletic footwear, apparel, and equipment company. Founded in 1964 and headquartered in Beaverton, Oregon, NIKE designs, develops, markets, and sells athletic and casual footwear, apparel, and accessories under the **NIKE**, **Jordan Brand**, **Converse**, and other subsidiary brands. The company operates globally across North America, Europe, Middle East & Africa (EMEA), Greater China, and Asia Pacific & Latin America (APLA). NIKE operates both through wholesale/retail channels and its Direct-to-Consumer (DTC) business, including digital platforms.
