The neutral analyst's strongest synthesis is that the modal June 30 outcome is neither a clean beat nor a clean miss but an ambiguous print (gross margin ~39.5-41%, in-line revenue, non-committal guidance) that produces a slow grind rather than a clean resolution — precisely the scenario where a noise-prone $40.40 stop fires on a grinding move and a starter holder faces the worst psychological setup. With the single metric that resolves the core dispute (Q4 gross-margin direction) and the dividend-credibility signal both landing in under 30 days, committing aggressively in either direction is unwarranted. This is the textbook balanced setup that calls for maintaining existing exposure and letting the catalyst do the work.
