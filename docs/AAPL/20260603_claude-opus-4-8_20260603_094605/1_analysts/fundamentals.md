## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Apple Inc. (AAPL)** is the world's most valuable publicly traded company by market capitalization. Founded in 1976 and headquartered in Cupertino, California, Apple designs, manufactures, and markets consumer electronics, software, and services. Its product ecosystem includes iPhone, Mac, iPad, Apple Watch, AirPods, and a rapidly growing services segment (App Store, Apple Music, iCloud, Apple Pay, Apple TV+). Apple's deeply integrated hardware-software-services model creates exceptional customer retention and a high-margin, recurring-revenue engine.

## 8. 🔍 Key Risks & Considerations

1. **AI Monetization Uncertainty:** Apple is investing heavily in "Apple Intelligence" AI features. R&D is accelerating sharply (+33% YoY), but monetization timelines are uncertain.
2. **Geopolitical/Supply Chain Risk:** Apple's manufacturing is heavily concentrated in China/Asia. Trade policy changes, tariffs, or geopolitical tensions remain a structural risk.
3. **Valuation Premium:** At 37.7x TTM P/E and a 4.57T market cap, the stock is priced for sustained excellence — any earnings miss or guidance cut could prompt a sharp de-rating.
4. **Regulatory Headwinds:** EU Digital Markets Act, DOJ antitrust suit (App Store), and ongoing global regulatory scrutiny on Apple's ecosystem lock-in.
5. **Services Regulation:** Apple's App Store commission rates face ongoing legal and regulatory challenges globally, threatening a key high-margin revenue stream.
6. **FX Headwinds:** ~60%+ of revenue is international; USD strength can meaningfully impact reported results.
