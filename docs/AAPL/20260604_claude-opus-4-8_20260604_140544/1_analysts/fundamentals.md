## 1. 📋 Company Profile

Apple Inc. is the world's largest publicly traded company by market capitalization, with a current market cap of approximately **$4.57 trillion**. Apple's vertically integrated hardware-software model and entrenched ecosystem create powerful switching costs and extraordinary pricing power.
