## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Overview

**Astera Labs, Inc. (ALAB)** is a fabless semiconductor company specializing in cloud-scale connectivity solutions — specifically purpose-built connectivity ICs (integrated circuits), modules, and software for AI and cloud infrastructure. Its product suite centers around **PCIe retimers, CXL memory interconnect controllers, Ethernet solutions, and smart cable modules** — all critical components in accelerating modern AI/ML and cloud data center workloads.

Astera Labs went public in **March 2024** via IPO, raising approximately $672M in its offering, and has since become one of the most closely-watched AI semiconductor plays given its close positioning in the hyperscaler and cloud data center supply chain.
