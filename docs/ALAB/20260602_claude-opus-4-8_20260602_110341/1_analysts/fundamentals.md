## 1. Company Profile & Overview

**Astera Labs, Inc. (ALAB)** is a fabless semiconductor company specializing in purpose-built connectivity solutions for AI and cloud infrastructure. The company designs and manufactures high-speed interconnect ICs (integrated circuits) — including PCIe, CXL, and Ethernet retimers and smart cable modules — that address bandwidth and power bottlenecks in hyperscale data centers. Astera's products are deeply embedded in AI accelerator racks, GPU server platforms, and next-generation data center architectures deployed by major cloud service providers (CSPs).

**Key Business Highlights:**
- Focuses on the explosive AI infrastructure buildout trend, supplying mission-critical semiconductor connectivity components
- Primarily a fabless model (outsourced manufacturing), maintaining high gross margins
- Customers are predominantly hyperscalers and major OEM/ODM partners
- IPO'd in March 2024, raising ~$672M in equity capital
