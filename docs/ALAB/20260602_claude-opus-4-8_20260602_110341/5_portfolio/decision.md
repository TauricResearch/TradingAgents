Second, and more important, price has changed the risk-reward decisively. The single most damaging fact in the entire debate is the bull's own EV math: only ~1% expected return at ~$320 under conservative multiples, requiring 90-100x forward P/E persistence to clear — a multiple no semiconductor name has sustained through a cycle. That is not asymmetry in the holder's favor on a triple-beta stock. The conservative and neutral analysts established four measurable conditions that have deteriorated since the $220 entry: (1) four consecutive quarters of sequential deceleration (20.4%→20.1%→17.3%→13.9%); (2) a confirmed — not theoretical — AR anomaly (62% QoQ on 14% revenue growth, a 4.4x mismatch); (3) technical extension/fatigue (78% in 18 sessions, 64.7% above the 50 SMA, RSI rolling 83.8→70.3, MACD histogram contracting 10.51→7.32); and (4) a sector-specialist (Northland) downgrade flagging 2027 capex risk, where multiple compression can occur on good numbers via narrowed expectation horizons. The aggressive analyst's recurring error — repeatedly conceding each of these and never changing the recommendation by a single share — conflates business quality with appropriate position size; the neutral analyst correctly named these as distinct questions. The conservative analyst's 25-30% trim, however, is internally inconsistent: if a $320→$250 gap is truly a central scenario, that trim doesn't protect against it, and if it isn't, the trim is oversized. The neutral analyst's resolution is the most coherent: a ~20% trim is mechanical recalibration to the original risk-based sizing, harvests gains at elevated prices, and crucially funds redeployment into the $220-240 zone if Q2 disappoints — genuine optionality, not an uncertainty tax. The bull's "cognitive friction" objection to trimming-then-rebuilding inverts sound risk management, which accepts small identifiable costs to hedge large uncertain ones. Net: an outstanding business at a dangerous price with a confirmed balance-sheet anomaly heading into a binary catalyst warrants Hold with a modest defensive trim, not full exposure and not exit.
