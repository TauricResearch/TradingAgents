## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Astera Labs, Inc. (ALAB)** is a fabless semiconductor and connectivity solutions company headquartered in Santa Clara, California. The company designs purpose-built, cloud-scale connectivity ICs and software-driven solutions that address bottlenecks in data center infrastructure. Their product portfolio includes PCIe retimers, CXL memory interconnect solutions, and Aries/Leo series connectivity chips, which are critical in AI/ML accelerated computing workloads. ALAB went public in March 2024 and rapidly became a high-profile name in the AI infrastructure semiconductor space.

Key clientele includes major hyperscalers and cloud computing providers who are aggressively deploying AI infrastructure, making ALAB a direct beneficiary of secular AI CapEx growth.
