## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**QUALCOMM Incorporated (QCOM)** is a global leader in semiconductor and telecommunications technology. The company operates primarily through two business segments:
- **QCT (Qualcomm CDMA Technologies):** Designs and supplies integrated circuits and system software for mobile devices, IoT, automotive, and networking.
- **QTL (Qualcomm Technology Licensing):** Licenses its broad intellectual property portfolio — particularly around 3G, 4G/LTE, and 5G wireless standards — to device manufacturers worldwide.

QUALCOMM is a critical enabler of 5G infrastructure and handset chipsets globally, with deep partnerships with major OEMs including Apple, Samsung, and virtually every Android device maker. The company also has fast-growing exposure to automotive and PC/AI edge compute markets.
