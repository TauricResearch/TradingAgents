## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Overview

**QUALCOMM Incorporated (QCOM)** is a global leader in wireless technology innovation. The company designs and markets semiconductors, software, and services based on its 5G, 4G, and other wireless technology standards. Its two core business divisions are:

- **QCT (Qualcomm CDMA Technologies):** The semiconductor segment — designing chips for smartphones (Snapdragon), automotive platforms, IoT devices, and PCs.
- **QTL (Qualcomm Technology Licensing):** The licensing segment — collecting royalties from original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) who use Qualcomm's extensive IP portfolio in wireless devices.

QCOM has transformed its strategy beyond smartphones to include automotive, industrial IoT, PC/edge computing, and wearables — diversifying its revenue base amid mobile market saturation.
