## 1. Company Profile

Tesla, Inc. (TSLA) is a vertically integrated electric vehicle (EV) manufacturer and clean energy company headquartered in Austin, Texas. Tesla designs, develops, manufactures, leases, and sells electric vehicles, energy generation and storage systems, and related services and accessories. Its core automotive lineup includes the Model 3, Model Y, Model S, Model X, Cybertruck, and the recently scaled Robotaxi/FSD (Full Self-Driving) ecosystem. Tesla also operates a rapidly growing Energy Generation & Storage segment (Powerwall, Megapack) and a Services segment.
