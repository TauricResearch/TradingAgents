## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Tesla, Inc. (TSLA)** is a vertically integrated electric vehicle (EV) manufacturer and clean energy company headquartered in Austin, Texas. Tesla designs, develops, manufactures, leases, and sells electric vehicles, energy generation and storage systems, and related services. Its product lineup includes the Model 3, Model Y, Model S, Model X, Cybertruck, and Semi, with the Robotaxi and next-generation affordable vehicles in development. Beyond automotive, Tesla operates the Energy Generation & Storage segment (Powerwall, Megapack, solar products) and a Services segment (FSD software, Supercharging, insurance).
