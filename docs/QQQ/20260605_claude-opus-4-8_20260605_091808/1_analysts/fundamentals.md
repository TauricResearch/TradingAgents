**Invesco QQQ Trust** (ticker: `QQQ`) is one of the world's most widely traded and recognized Exchange-Traded Funds (ETFs). The fund is often used as a **proxy for the technology sector** and broad growth equity investing, though it also includes significant exposure to consumer discretionary, healthcare, industrials, and communication services.
