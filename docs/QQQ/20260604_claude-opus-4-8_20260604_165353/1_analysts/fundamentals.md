## 1. 🏢 Company / Fund Profile

The **Invesco QQQ Trust (QQQ)** is one of the most widely traded and recognized exchange-traded funds (ETFs) in the world. It tracks the **Nasdaq-100 Index**, which comprises the 100 largest non-financial companies listed on the Nasdaq Stock Market, weighted by market capitalization. The fund was launched in **March 1999** and is managed by **Invesco Ltd.**, a global investment management firm.

QQQ provides investors with **broad exposure to the technology, consumer discretionary, communication services, healthcare, and industrials sectors**, with a heavy concentration in mega-cap technology and growth-oriented companies. Notable underlying holdings typically include giants such as Apple (AAPL), Microsoft (MSFT), NVIDIA (NVDA), Amazon (AMZN), Meta Platforms (META), Alphabet (GOOGL/GOOG), and Tesla (TSLA), among others.

- **Dividend Policy:** Passes through dividends from underlying holdings on a quarterly basis
