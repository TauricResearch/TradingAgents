### What is QQQ?
The **Invesco QQQ Trust (QQQ)** is one of the largest and most actively traded ETFs in the world. It tracks the **NASDAQ-100 Index**, which comprises the 100 largest non-financial companies listed on the NASDAQ Stock Market, weighted by market capitalization. The fund provides exposure to high-growth, technology-dominant sectors including semiconductors, software, internet services, biotechnology, and consumer electronics.

**Key characteristics:**
- QQQ is a **passive vehicle** — it does not generate independent revenue, issue debt, or publish traditional corporate financial statements (balance sheet, income statement, cash flow). This explains the `NO_DATA_AVAILABLE` responses for those tools.
- Its "financials" are entirely a **reflection of its underlying portfolio** of 100 constituent stocks.
- The fund's **PE Ratio, Price-to-Book, Book Value**, etc., are aggregate weighted metrics derived from the holdings.
