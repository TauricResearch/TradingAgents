## 1. 🏛️ Company Profile & Overview

**Tencent Holdings Limited** is one of the world's largest technology conglomerates, headquartered in Shenzhen, China. It operates across multiple business verticals including:

- **Social Networking & Messaging:** WeChat (Weixin), QQ — among the world's most-used communication platforms
- **Online Gaming:** Domestic and international gaming (largest gaming company by revenue globally), with stakes in Riot Games, Epic Games, Supercell, and more
- **Fintech & Business Services:** WeChat Pay (one of China's dominant mobile payment systems), cloud services, enterprise SaaS
- **Digital Content & Media:** Tencent Video, Tencent Music, literature, animation, and sports streaming
- **Investments & Ventures:** Massive portfolio in global tech (Meituan, JD.com, Sea Limited, Snap, Spotify, Universal Music Group, etc.)

Tencent's ADR (`TCEHY`) trades on the OTC Pink Sheets in the U.S. market with each ADR representing approximately 1/10th of a Hong Kong-listed share (0700.HK).
