**Source: Bloomberg | Late May / Early June 2026**

Trump administration officials are debating whether a **regulatory loophole allowed Chinese firms to purchase Nvidia Blackwell chips** — the most advanced AI accelerators currently available. The internal debate reflects ongoing tension between export control enforcement and the practical challenge of policing global semiconductor supply chains.

**Significance for TCEHY:**
