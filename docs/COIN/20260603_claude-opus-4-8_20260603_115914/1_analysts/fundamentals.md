## 1. Company Profile

**Coinbase Global, Inc. (COIN)** is the largest publicly traded cryptocurrency exchange in the United States, offering retail and institutional clients the ability to buy, sell, and store digital assets. Beyond its core exchange function, Coinbase has expanded into staking, custody services, a stablecoin ecosystem (USDC), blockchain infrastructure, and most recently, institutional-grade DeFi and Layer-2 solutions. The company went public via direct listing in April 2021.
