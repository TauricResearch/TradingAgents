## 1. COMPANY PROFILE & OVERVIEW

Coinbase Global, Inc. (COIN) is the largest U.S.-regulated cryptocurrency exchange, operating a vertically integrated financial infrastructure platform for the digital asset economy. Its business encompasses retail and institutional trading, custodial services, staking, lending, blockchain infrastructure, and developer tools (via Base, its Layer 2 blockchain). Founded in 2012 and publicly listed in April 2021 via a direct listing on Nasdaq, Coinbase is uniquely positioned as the primary fiat-to-crypto on-ramp in the United States and increasingly global.

**Key Business Segments:**
- **Consumer/Retail Trading:** Transaction fees from retail users buying/selling crypto
- **Institutional Services:** Prime brokerage, custody, and OTC trading
- **Subscription & Services:** Staking rewards, interest income, USDC revenue, blockchain rewards
- **Coinbase One:** Premium subscription offering
- **Base (L2 Network):** Developer and DeFi infrastructure
