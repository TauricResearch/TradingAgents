The neutral analyst's synthesis is the most defensible operationalization: anchor the sizing decision to maximum tolerable drawdown at worst-case stop execution, not to a fixed trim percentage. That probability-weighted, flat-to-slightly-positive EV with enormous variance and a known negative catalyst directly ahead is precisely a Hold, not a Buy or a Sell.
