## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**CrowdStrike Holdings, Inc. (CRWD)** is a global cybersecurity leader headquartered in Austin, Texas. The company operates the Falcon platform — a cloud-native, AI-powered endpoint security and threat intelligence platform that has become central to enterprise security infrastructure worldwide. CrowdStrike serves government agencies, Fortune 500 corporations, and SMBs across more than 170 countries. Its business model is predominantly subscription-based (SaaS), generating highly recurring and predictable revenues through annual recurring revenue (ARR) contracts.

**Core Business Segments:**
- Endpoint Security (EDR/XDR)
- Cloud Security & Workload Protection
- Identity Threat Protection
- Threat Intelligence (Falcon Intelligence)
- Security Operations (Falcon Complete MDR)
- Observability and Log Management (Falcon LogScale)
