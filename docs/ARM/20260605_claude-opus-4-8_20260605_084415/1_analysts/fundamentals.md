## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Arm Holdings plc (NASDAQ: ARM)** is the world's foremost semiconductor IP (intellectual property) licensing company. Rather than manufacturing chips, ARM designs the core processor architectures (primarily RISC-based ARM instruction set architectures) that power the vast majority of mobile devices (smartphones, tablets), embedded systems, IoT devices, server processors, and increasingly AI accelerators and data center infrastructure.

ARM's business model is IP-centric: chipmakers (Apple, Qualcomm, NVIDIA, MediaTek, Amazon, etc.) license ARM's architecture to design chips, paying upfront **license fees** and ongoing **royalties** per chip shipped. This asset-light model generates exceptional gross margins. ARM was re-listed on NASDAQ in September 2023 after being taken private by SoftBank in 2016.
