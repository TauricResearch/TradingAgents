## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Arm Holdings plc (NASDAQ: ARM)** is a British semiconductor and software design company headquartered in Cambridge, United Kingdom. As the world's dominant processor architecture licensor, ARM designs the instruction set architectures (ISAs) and microprocessor IP that power the vast majority of mobile devices, embedded systems, IoT endpoints, automotive chips, servers, and increasingly, AI accelerators. ARM does not manufacture chips itself — its business model is IP licensing: charging royalties and licensing fees to semiconductor companies (Apple, Qualcomm, Samsung, NVIDIA, MediaTek, etc.) who use ARM's architecture to design and fabricate their chips.

- **Sector:** Technology
- **Industry:** Semiconductors
- **Business Model:** Fabless IP Licensing + Royalties
- **Market Capitalization:** ~$433.2 billion
- **Parent/Major Shareholder:** SoftBank Group (majority owner post-IPO)
