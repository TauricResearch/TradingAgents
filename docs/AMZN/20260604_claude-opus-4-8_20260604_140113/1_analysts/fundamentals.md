- **Advertising Services** – High-margin digital advertising leveraging Amazon's first-party data
- **Subscription Services** – Amazon Prime, music, video streaming
- **Physical Stores** – Whole Foods and Amazon Fresh
- **Devices & Hardware** – Alexa, Kindle, Ring, Fire TV

The company commands a **market capitalization of ~$2.73 trillion**, cementing its position among the world's most valuable publicly traded companies.
