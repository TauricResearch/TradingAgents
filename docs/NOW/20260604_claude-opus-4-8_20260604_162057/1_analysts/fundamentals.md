## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**ServiceNow, Inc.** is a global enterprise cloud computing company headquartered in Santa Clara, California. It provides a cloud-based platform — the **Now Platform** — that digitizes workflows and automates routine tasks across IT service management (ITSM), HR, customer service, finance, and security operations. ServiceNow's platform is mission-critical for large enterprises, governments, and financial institutions, creating high switching costs and deeply entrenched customer relationships.

ServiceNow's primary monetization model is **subscription-based SaaS**, generating predictable, recurring revenue. Its product portfolio spans ITSM, IT Operations Management (ITOM), Customer Service Management (CSM), HR Service Delivery (HRSD), and increasingly, **AI/generative AI-powered workflows** through its "AI Agents" and "Now Assist" product lines.
