## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**ServiceNow, Inc.** is a global enterprise cloud software company headquartered in Santa Clara, California. ServiceNow operates a single-tenant and multi-tenant cloud-based platform — the Now Platform — that enables organizations to digitize and automate workflows across IT, employee experience, customer operations, risk management, and more. The company primarily generates revenue through subscription contracts, with professional services and training as secondary revenue streams. ServiceNow serves thousands of enterprises worldwide, including a significant proportion of the Fortune 500, and is widely regarded as a category-defining platform for enterprise workflow automation.
