## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Applied Materials, Inc. (AMAT)** is the world's largest manufacturer of semiconductor wafer fabrication equipment and related services. Founded in 1967 and headquartered in Santa Clara, California, the company provides critical manufacturing tools, processes, and services to the global semiconductor, display, and adjacent industries. AMAT's products are essential to the production of chips used in smartphones, data centers, automotive applications, AI processors, and nearly every modern electronic device.

**Key Business Segments:**
- **Semiconductor Systems** – Equipment for wafer fabrication (deposition, etch, CMP, metrology, etc.)
- **Applied Global Services (AGS)** – Maintenance, parts, and upgrades for installed equipment
- **Display & Adjacent Markets** – Equipment for flat-panel display manufacturing

AMAT holds a dominant position in key semiconductor process steps and benefits enormously from the secular growth trend in AI infrastructure investment, leading-edge node transitions (e.g., gate-all-around, 2nm and below), and ongoing global fab build-out.
