## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Applied Materials, Inc. (AMAT)** is the world's largest supplier of semiconductor manufacturing equipment and materials. The company designs, manufactures, and services equipment used in the fabrication of integrated circuits (ICs), flat panel displays, and solar photovoltaic cells. AMAT serves virtually every major chipmaker globally, including TSMC, Samsung, Intel, and ASML ecosystem customers.
