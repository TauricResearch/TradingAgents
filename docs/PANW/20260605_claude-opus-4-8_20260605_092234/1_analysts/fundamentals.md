## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

Palo Alto Networks, Inc. (PANW) is the world's leading cybersecurity platform company. Headquartered in Santa Clara, California, it delivers cloud-delivered security services, next-generation firewalls, endpoint protection, and AI-driven security operations. PANW has pursued a **platformization strategy**, consolidating disparate cybersecurity tools into unified platforms — Strata (network security), Prisma (cloud security), and Cortex (AI-driven SecOps). This strategy is a central driver of long-term customer value and retention, as evidenced by massive deferred revenue growth.
