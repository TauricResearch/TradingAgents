When the bull case rests on demonstrated fundamentals and the bear case rests largely on entry timing, valuation discipline, and a not-yet-visible competitive/integration risk, the stronger side is the bull — but not at full conviction, because the bear is right that the price leaves zero room for error and the entry matters enormously here.

So: constructive, not euphoric. Overweight, not Buy.
