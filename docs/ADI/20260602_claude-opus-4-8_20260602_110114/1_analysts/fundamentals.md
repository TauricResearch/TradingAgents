## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Business Overview

**Analog Devices, Inc. (ADI)** is a global semiconductor powerhouse headquartered in Wilmington, Massachusetts. Founded in 1965, ADI specializes in the design, manufacturing, and marketing of high-performance analog, mixed-signal, and digital signal processing (DSP) integrated circuits. ADI serves critical end markets including industrial automation, automotive electronics, communications infrastructure, healthcare devices, and consumer electronics.

Key strategic pillars:
- **Industrial segment**: Factory automation, instrumentation, energy
- **Automotive segment**: Electric vehicles, ADAS systems
- **Communications**: 5G infrastructure, wireline networks
- **Digital healthcare**: Monitoring, imaging, diagnostics

ADI completed the landmark **$21B acquisition of Maxim Integrated** in 2021, significantly expanding its product portfolio, deepening penetration in automotive and industrial IoT, and creating substantial goodwill (~$26.9B on balance sheet).
