## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Overview

**Analog Devices, Inc. (ADI)** is a global leader in high-performance analog, mixed-signal, and digital signal processing (DSP) integrated circuits. Founded in 1965 and headquartered in Wilmington, Massachusetts, ADI serves markets including industrial automation, automotive, communications, healthcare, and consumer electronics. Its product portfolio encompasses data converters, amplifiers, RF/microwave components, power management ICs, and intelligent edge computing solutions.

ADI's scale and diversification — spanning industrial (typically ~50%+ of revenue), automotive, communications, and consumer end markets — provide resilience across semiconductor cycles. The company's 2021 acquisition of Maxim Integrated (~$21B) meaningfully expanded its portfolio and scale, adding power management and embedded sensing capabilities.
