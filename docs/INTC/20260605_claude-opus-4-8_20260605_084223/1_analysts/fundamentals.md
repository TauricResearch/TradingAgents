## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Intel Corporation (INTC)** is one of the world's largest semiconductor companies, headquartered in Santa Clara, California. Intel designs and manufactures microprocessors, chipsets, network adapters, storage devices, and AI accelerators. The company operates through key segments including Client Computing Group (CCG), Data Center and AI (DCAI), Network and Edge (NEX), and Intel Foundry Services (IFS). Intel is undergoing one of the most significant strategic transformations in its history — pivoting toward IDM 2.0 (Integrated Device Manufacturer), expanding its foundry business, and attempting to reclaim technological leadership in semiconductor manufacturing processes.
