## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Intel Corporation (INTC)** is one of the world's largest semiconductor companies, headquartered in Santa Clara, California. Intel designs and manufactures microprocessors, chipsets, system-on-chip (SoC) products, networking components, FPGAs (via its Intel Foundry division, formerly including Altera), storage solutions, and AI/data center accelerators. The company operates across major business segments including:

- **Client Computing Group (CCG):** Desktop and laptop processors
- **Data Center and AI (DCAI):** Server CPUs, AI accelerators
- **Network and Edge (NEX):** Network infrastructure
- **Intel Foundry Services (IFS):** Third-party chip manufacturing (IDM 2.0 strategy)
- **Mobileye:** Advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS; partially divested)

Intel is currently in a **major strategic transformation**, attempting to rebuild process leadership with its Intel 18A and 3nm node technologies while simultaneously launching a foundry services business to compete with TSMC and Samsung.
