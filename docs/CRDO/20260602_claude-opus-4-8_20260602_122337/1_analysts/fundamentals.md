## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Overview

**Credo Technology Group Holding Ltd (CRDO)** is a high-growth semiconductor company specializing in **high-speed connectivity solutions** designed for AI/ML data centers, cloud infrastructure, and high-performance computing (HPC) environments. The company's core product families include **Active Electrical Cables (AECs)**, **SerDes (serializer/deserializer) IP**, **optical DSPs**, and **line-card PHY solutions**, which enable extreme-bandwidth connectivity between servers, switches, and storage systems at dramatically lower power consumption than traditional optical transceiver solutions.

Credo is headquartered in San Jose, California, with significant engineering operations in Asia. The company went public on NASDAQ in January 2022 and has since emerged as one of the fastest-growing pure-play connectivity semiconductor companies globally, benefiting directly from surging AI infrastructure buildouts.
