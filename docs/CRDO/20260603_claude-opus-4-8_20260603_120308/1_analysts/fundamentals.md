## 1. Company Profile

**Credo Technology Group Holding Ltd** (`CRDO`) is a fabless semiconductor company specializing in high-speed connectivity solutions for data centers, hyperscalers, and AI/ML infrastructure. The company designs and markets Active Electrical Cables (AECs), SerDes chiplets, line card PHYs, and optical digital signal processors (DSPs) that enable high-bandwidth, low-latency, and power-efficient interconnects. Credo's customer base is concentrated among Tier-1 hyperscalers (cloud and AI compute providers), with solutions deployed in 400G/800G/1.6T Ethernet networking environments. The company is headquartered in San Jose, California, and went public on Nasdaq in January 2022.

**Key Segments:** Connectivity ICs, Active Electrical Cables (AECs), Optical DSPs, and IP licensing/NRE.
