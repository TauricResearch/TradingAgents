## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Micron Technology, Inc. (MU)** is one of the world's leading semiconductor companies, specializing in the design, manufacturing, and sale of memory and storage products. Its primary product lines include DRAM (Dynamic Random-Access Memory), NAND Flash, and NOR Flash storage, serving markets that span data centers, AI/ML workloads, mobile devices, automotive applications, and consumer electronics.

Micron is one of only three companies globally (alongside Samsung and SK Hynix) capable of producing advanced DRAM at scale, giving it a critical and irreplaceable role in the global semiconductor supply chain. With AI infrastructure buildouts accelerating demand for High-Bandwidth Memory (HBM) and high-capacity DDR5, Micron is exceptionally well-positioned for the current technological cycle.
