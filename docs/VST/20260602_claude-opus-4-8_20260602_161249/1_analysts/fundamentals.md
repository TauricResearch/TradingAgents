## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Overview

Vistra Corp. (NYSE: VST) is one of the largest competitive power generators and retail energy providers in the United States. Headquartered in Irving, Texas, Vistra operates across multiple electricity markets including ERCOT (Texas), PJM, MISO, and CAISO. The company controls a diverse fleet of generation assets spanning natural gas, coal, nuclear, solar, and battery storage. Vistra's retail electricity brand, TXU Energy, is one of the largest retail providers in Texas.

The company occupies a unique position in the utility space as an **Independent Power Producer (IPP)**, meaning it operates in competitive, deregulated power markets rather than under traditional regulated utility pricing. This structure creates higher earnings volatility but also enables Vistra to capture upside from wholesale power price spikes — particularly relevant in an era of AI-driven power demand, electrification trends, and data center buildout.
