## 1. Company Profile

Arista Networks, Inc. is a leading provider of cloud networking solutions for data-center, campus, and routing environments. The company develops and sells multilayer network switches and software-defined networking (SDN) platforms, offering its flagship Extensible Operating System (EOS) that spans the full product portfolio. Arista's primary customers include large cloud hyperscalers (Meta, Microsoft), financial institutions, and enterprises globally. The company competes principally with Cisco Systems, Juniper Networks, and emerging white-box switch vendors.
