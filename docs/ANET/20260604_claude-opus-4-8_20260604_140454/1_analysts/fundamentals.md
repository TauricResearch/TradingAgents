## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Arista Networks, Inc. (ANET)** is a leading provider of cloud networking solutions for large-scale data centers, campus environments, and routing infrastructure. The company's products are built on its proprietary **Extensible Operating System (EOS)**, a single, consistent network OS underpinning its entire hardware portfolio. Arista's primary customers include major cloud titans (often referred to as "Cloud Titans"), enterprise customers, and service providers. The company competes primarily with Cisco Systems, Juniper Networks, and Huawei in the data center networking space.

With the explosive growth of AI/ML workloads driving demand for high-bandwidth, low-latency networking, Arista has emerged as a critical infrastructure enabler — particularly in 400G and 800G Ethernet switching, which is being adopted at scale by hyperscalers deploying GPU-dense AI clusters.
