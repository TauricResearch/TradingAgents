## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Business Overview

Microsoft Corporation is one of the world's largest technology companies by market capitalization, headquartered in Redmond, Washington. The company operates across three primary business segments:

- **Productivity and Business Processes** – Office 365, Microsoft 365, Teams, LinkedIn, Dynamics 365
- **Intelligent Cloud** – Azure cloud platform, SQL Server, Windows Server, GitHub, Nuance
- **More Personal Computing** – Windows OS, Xbox, Surface devices, Bing search and advertising

Microsoft's strategic transformation under CEO Satya Nadella has positioned Azure cloud and AI services (including deep integration with OpenAI) as primary growth engines. The Activision Blizzard acquisition (~$69B in FY2024) further extended its gaming footprint.
