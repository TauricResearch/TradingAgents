Net: the strongest arguments favor accumulating the position because the business quality and 3-5 year thesis are compelling and largely uncontested. But the bear's discipline argument on entry is well-supported enough that I won't endorse aggressive buying into a confirmed-overbought, sell-the-news technical setup. That combination — constructive conviction tempered by a stretched entry — is precisely what Overweight is for: build the position gradually and on weakness rather than chasing.
