## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Microsoft Corporation** is one of the world's largest technology companies, headquartered in Redmond, Washington. It develops, licenses, and supports a wide range of software products, services, devices, and solutions. The company operates across three principal segments:

Microsoft has become a dominant force in cloud computing through Azure, enterprise software through Microsoft 365, and AI infrastructure via its strategic investment in OpenAI. The company is at the center of the generative AI megatrend, embedding Copilot AI across nearly all its product lines.

1. **Azure AI Services** — Azure continues to be the #2 cloud provider globally and is growing faster than AWS in AI workloads. Microsoft's exclusive partnership with OpenAI gives it a first-mover advantage in enterprise AI.
2. **Microsoft Copilot** — Embedded across Microsoft 365, Teams, GitHub, Dynamics, and Azure, representing upsell potential across its massive enterprise customer base.
