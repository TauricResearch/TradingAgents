## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Business Overview

Palantir Technologies Inc. (PLTR) is a leading data analytics and AI software platform company founded in 2003 and headquartered in Denver, Colorado. Its core product suite spans two flagship platforms:

- **Palantir Gotham** – Government intelligence and defense analytics
- **Palantir Foundry** – Commercial enterprise data integration and analytics
- **Palantir AIP (Artificial Intelligence Platform)** – Its newest and highest-growth driver, enabling large language models (LLMs) and AI agents to act on real-world operational data

PLTR serves a dual-market model: a foundational U.S. Government segment (defense, intelligence, law enforcement) and a rapidly growing U.S. and International Commercial segment. The AIP platform has become a critical catalyst for commercial customer acquisition since its 2023 launch, significantly accelerating revenue growth.
