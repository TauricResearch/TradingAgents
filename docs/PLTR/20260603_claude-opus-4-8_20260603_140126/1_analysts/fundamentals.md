## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

Palantir Technologies Inc. (ticker: **PLTR**) is a leading American software company specializing in big data analytics, AI-powered decision intelligence, and data integration platforms. Founded in 2003 and headquartered in Denver, Colorado, Palantir serves two primary markets:

- **Government/Defense:** Platforms including Gotham (intelligence/defense analytics) and Foundry/AIP (AI Platform) for national security agencies, defense contractors, and government bodies.
- **Commercial/Enterprise:** Foundry and AIP platforms serving large enterprises across healthcare, finance, energy, manufacturing, and other industries.

The company has accelerated its commercial growth in recent years, particularly through its **Artificial Intelligence Platform (AIP)**, which has become a major growth driver since 2023. As of Q1 2026, Palantir has established itself as a premier enterprise AI platform provider with substantial recurring revenue, a fortress-like balance sheet, and dramatically improving profitability.
