## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Palantir Technologies Inc. (PLTR)** is a leading AI-powered software and data analytics platform company headquartered in Denver, Colorado. Originally founded in 2003, Palantir develops and deploys decision-intelligence software for government agencies and large commercial enterprises. Its primary platforms include:

- **Palantir Gotham** – Intelligence/government mission analytics
- **Palantir Foundry** – Enterprise data integration and operations
- **Palantir AIP (AI Platform)** – AI-native workflow automation and large language model (LLM) orchestration
- **Apollo** – Continuous delivery and software deployment infrastructure

The company operates in two major segments: **Government** (U.S. federal agencies, defense, intelligence) and **Commercial** (enterprise clients globally). PLTR is increasingly positioning itself as the premier "AI operating system" for both public and private sector clients — a differentiated market niche with high switching costs and deep institutional integrations.
